The Story of Amplified


The Idea

Samir came up with the idea after hearing countless students complain about their learning environments, and how little say they have in their education.

We wanted to create something that gave students a powerful voice, but more importantly a constructive one. The last thing we wanted to do was make a bad situation worse by creating a 'twitter like' rant page. We wanted unbaised blogs that would spark educated and constructive discussions to find solutions.

The Start

Samir then brought it up while in programming class together, and I was hooked. I agreed with his idea and suggested we get started right away. That night we began, starting by learning the programming languages of the web which we quickly reaized were going to be imperative to our success. Although both of our roles overlaped with each other, Samir was the mastermind behind how the infrasturcture would work while I was the brain behind the website. Toghther, we were able to create a website that was so aesthetically pleasing that when we presented to one of the school board members, they were amazed what two kids could do.
